Test Flow — How to use these QRs

  1. Scan Entry QR first — activates the exhibition on your Home screen dashboard.
  2. Scan Booth QRs from the same exhibition — saved brands appear under that show in your Saved tab.
  3. Scan People QRs (any time) — saved contacts appear in your Connections tab.
  4. Switch exhibitions by scanning a different Entry QR — the active show updates on Home.
  5. Tap any QR to enlarge it for easier phone scanning.
